About Comfort PG

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Comfort PG started with a simple mission: to provide comfortable, affordable, and safe accommodation for students and working professionals. What began as a small venture has now grown into a trusted home away from home for many.

Our Mission

We aim to create a supportive living environment that nurtures personal growth, provides top-notch facilities, and ensures the safety and comfort of our residents. Our goal is to make your stay as convenient and enjoyable as possible.

Why Choose Us?

  • Hygienic and well-maintained premises
  • 24/7 security and CCTV surveillance
  • Nutritious and timely meals
  • High-speed Wi-Fi
  • Laundry and housekeeping services
